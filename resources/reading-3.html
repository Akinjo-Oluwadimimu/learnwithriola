Lessons 11 - 15

Learn by Reading and Listening

Xiànzài jǐ diǎn？

现 在 几 点?

What's the time now?

       Xiànzài jǐ diǎn？
A：现 在  几  点?

A：What's the time now?

      Xiànzài shí diǎn shí fēn
B：现 在  十  点  十  分.

B：It's ten past ten.

       Zhōngwǔ jǐ diǎn chī fàn？
A：中 午  几  点  吃 饭?

A：When shall we have our lunch?

      Shí'èr diǎn chī fàn 
B：十 二  点  吃 饭.

B：At twelve o'clock.

New Words

1. 现在 xiànzài noun. now

2. 点 diǎn measure. o'clock

3. 分 fēn measure. minute

4. 中午 zhōngwǔ noun. noon

5. 吃饭 chī fàn verb. to eat a meal
