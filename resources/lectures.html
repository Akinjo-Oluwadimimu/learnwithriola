learning made fun and easy

Riola offers various learning processes which range from chinese language reading, chinese character writing, chinese poems recitation to fun games which help to keep track of your progress and boost your knowledge and comprehension skills.

To further help you know your way around this learning platform, the following are tips on how to work around the platform.

Our reading programs are categorized into fifteen lessons. Each lesson consists of three texts. The audio files for each text have been provided in order to help you grasp the correct pronunciations of the words, phrases and sentences used in the texts.

Riola provides the detailed stroke order of all chinese characters used in the reading and listening sections of learning. The characters are categorized alphabetically and can be accessed by clicking the writing menu in the navigation bar.

Click on Poems in the navigation bar to check out contemporary Chinese poems

Riola also provides a wide range of games including pop quizzes. Select Games on the navigation bar to check them out.
